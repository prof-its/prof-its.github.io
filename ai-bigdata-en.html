The programme aims to train specialists able to solve complex engineering and information problems in AI, big data analytics and electronic communications, and to develop, integrate and operate intelligent info‑telecom systems and services.

What you will study (highlights)

key topics
  • Telecom and computer system architectures; networks and cloud infrastructures.
  • Mathematical and algorithmic foundations of AI/ML and big data analytics.
  • Distributed, embedded and cloud/edge systems; virtualization and containerization.
  • Intelligent monitoring, optimization and management of networks and computing resources.
  • Security of information and communication systems.
