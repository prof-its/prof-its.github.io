The programme trains specialists able to design, implement and operate communication, control and telemetry systems for UAS, considering real‑time constraints, safety, reliability and interference immunity.

What you will study (highlights)

key topics
  • Control, telemetry and data transmission channels for unmanned aerial systems.
  • Navigation and inertial navigation systems; satellite communications and navigation.
  • Automatic control systems; modelling of communication channels and control loops.
  • Integration of onboard and ground information and communication subsystems.
  • Reliability, interference immunity and secure data exchange in real operation.
