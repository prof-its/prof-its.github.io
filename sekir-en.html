What you will study (highlights)

key topics
  • Architectures and principles of electronic communication systems and IoT.
  • Design and operation of IoT solutions, networks and services.
  • Network technologies and protocols, including IP‑based approaches.
  • Monitoring and management tools for electronic communication and IoT networks.
  • Practice‑oriented components: labs, coursework and projects.
