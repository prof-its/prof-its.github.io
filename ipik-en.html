The programme trains specialists who can solve engineering and programming problems in infocommunications under complex and uncertain conditions, with an emphasis on innovation and modern network technologies.

What you will study (highlights)

key topics
  • Telecommunication and radio systems: principles, modelling and engineering design.
  • Infocom engineering: hardware‑software platforms and network services.
  • Programming for infocom solutions, including software‑oriented approaches.
  • Modern infocom technologies, including mobile infocommunications.
  • Monitoring, control and maintenance processes in telecom systems.
