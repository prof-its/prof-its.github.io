About the Institute

overview

Bachelor education is delivered through three departments: Telecommunications (TK), Information Technologies in Telecommunications (ITT), and Electronic Communications and the Internet of Things (ECIoT).

This website is a concise guide to five programmes for applicants and guests of the outreach event.
