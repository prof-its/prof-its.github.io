The programme prepares specialists capable of complex problem solving in ICT and the development and improvement of information and telecommunication technologies for technical facilities and networks.

What you will study (highlights)

key topics
  • Networked technologies for processing, storing and exchanging information at a distance.
  • Principles and models of electronic communication systems and networks.
  • Methods for achieving required operational characteristics (reliability, quality, efficiency).
  • Modern software/hardware platforms used in electronic communications and digital systems.
  • Monitoring and control tools for communication processes and services.
